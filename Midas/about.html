About Us

Founded in 2008, Midas Auto Services Centre Limited has grown from a bold idea into Kenya's most trusted road rescue and automotive logistics company. What started as a single tow truck in Nairobi has evolved into a nationwide operation spanning all 47 counties — with physical offices in Nairobi, Nakuru, Kisumu, Eldoret, Nyeri, Mombasa, Voi, and Tsavo serving clients around the clock.

As a Founder Member of the Automobile Towing and Recovery Association (ATRA) and a pioneer provider for the Government of Kenya fleet leasing program, we bring the same level of dedication to every client — whether it's a corporate fleet of 500 vehicles or a stranded motorist on the Nairobi-Nakuru highway at 2 AM. Our response times are among the fastest in the industry, and our technicians are equipped to handle everything from a simple flat tire to complex multi-vehicle recovery operations.

Midas currently serves both corporate and non-corporate clients across the country. We are committed to providing a proficient, effective, swift and prompt level of response whilst integrating quality and support to all our customers. Our service is known for its reliability and fast turnaround times.
