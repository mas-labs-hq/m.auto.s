Who We Are

About Midas
Auto Services

Founded in 2008, Midas Auto Services Centre Limited is Kenya's premier road rescue and automotive logistics company. With over 18 years of proven excellence, we have grown from a small Nairobi-based operation into a nationwide enterprise with physical offices spanning the country — from our Nairobi headquarters to branches in Nakuru, Kisumu, Eldoret, Nyeri, Mombasa, Voi, and Tsavo. We proudly serve both corporate organizations and individual motorists, delivering swift, professional, and dependable roadside assistance whenever and wherever it's needed.
